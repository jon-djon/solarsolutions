  Commercial Window Film

*** 10 or 12 year Warranty On All Commercial Window Film! ***

Business owners and building managers everywhere are taking advantage of the countless benefits that today's advanced technology window films have to offer. Installed by experienced professionals without disrupting your business routine. Window films start paying off from day one and make a big difference on your bottom line.
  Increased Energy Efficiency

All year window film is at work helping to reduce your energy bills. Select films reject as much as 82% of the sun's total energy for more efficient cooling in the summer. Window film also insulates glass, helping retain heat in the winter months. So year round, you are spending less money to power the company! Plus, reducing your operating expenses increases your property value - an added benefit when selling or leasing.
  Improved Comfort and Workplace Productivity


Increased comfort begins as soon as window film is installed. It immediately reduces or eliminates uncomfortable hot spots and minimizes annoying glare that "washes out" computer screens, which can lead to valuable improvement in employee productivity. Best of all, building temperatures are easier to regulate and maintain, and that results in happier tenants, business owners and managers.
  Reduced Fading

The sun's ultraviolet light is a major contributor to fading and deterioration of draperies, carpets, furnishings, storefront displays and other interior items. All window films reject almost 100% of this harmful and damaging ultraviolet light, protecting a building's contents from premature damage and occupants from any ultraviolet health threat.
  Improved Appearance

Window films add elegance to all buildings, storefronts and restaurants, regardless of architectural style. The wide variety of colors and shades compliment any building's interior and exterior design. Window film is the ideal retrofit solution to give older structures a contemporary look and give new construction an eye-catching finish. Best of all, improved appearance is just one of the countless benefits that window film has to offer.

  Greater Safety

Accidents can happen any time, but window film helps reduce the risk of glass related injuries by increasing the windows' shatter-resistance. Although not specifically designed to be a safety film, all standard products are installed with a strong adhesive system that bonds to the glass. This makes glass stronger and more shatter resistant - so, if an accident happens, the glass is more likely to stay in place, reducing the threat caused by airborne shards of broken glass.
  Easy Maintenance

From the inside, window film has a pleasant, glare and distortion-free view and from the outside it has a uniform appearance that complements the building's design. And, window film is available in a range of designer shades, including neutral. Scratch-resistant coating resists scratches, keeping the film looking as new as the day it was installed assuring carefree maintenance with most conventional methods of window cleaning.
